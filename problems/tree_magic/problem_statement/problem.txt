Statement
---
Harry is working on a magic potion, this potion have a spreading factor equal to 2, it works by throwing it on one opponent ,
 the magic will spread to 2 other opponents each time one of them is infected, this spreading will go on for **g** generation, each opponent will be injured with a magic damage equals to the number of the generation he is part of, Harry wants to know the sum of all the damage he dealt to all his  opponents 

ps: the very first infected opponent is part of the generation 1

input
---
g: the number of generations

output
---
the sum of all the damage he dealt


example
---
- input: 3
- output:  17
