Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker is going to be premiered in your city and EVERYONE want to watch it, but the places are so limited !!!.
Your local cinema introduced a virtual queue to avoid the chaos, this allows users to pre-order tickets at their website once they go on sale.
given a number n, print the sequence of n steps in which the queue gets filled starting from the pre-order of the first ticket.

INPUT:
A single integer,n, denoting the size of the sequence.

OUTPUT:
Print the sequence of n steps, using 0s and 1s. Observe that it's base and height are both equal to n.

Sample Input: 
4
Sample Output:
0001
0011
0111
1111
