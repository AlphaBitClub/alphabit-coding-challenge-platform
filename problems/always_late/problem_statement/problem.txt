Input:
---
1st line: contains 4 space separated integers (N, T, C, D)
N the number of train stations
T train speed (km/h)
C car speed (km/h)
D delay time in (min)
the next N lines: each contains a single integer S_i the distance of the ith station

Task:
---
Print the station number which you should get off at,
where the your wait time is minimal


Constraint:
---
D > 0
C > T
